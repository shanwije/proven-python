# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

This project follows the
[Contributor Covenant, version 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).
In short: everyone is welcome here, and we keep this a respectful, harassment-free space for
people of every background and identity.

We expect contributors to be considerate, constructive, and patient. Harassment, personal
attacks, and other abusive or disruptive behavior are not tolerated, in issues, pull requests,
discussions, or any other project space.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness behavior that breaks this standard, report it privately to the
maintainer at `mail@shanwijenayaka.com`. Reports are handled confidentially, and the maintainer
will respond as promptly as they reasonably can.

## Enforcement

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this standard. They may remove, edit,
or reject contributions, comments, or other content that does not align with it, and may
temporarily or permanently ban any contributor for behavior they judge harmful. The full
enforcement guidelines, including scope and the response ladder, are in the
[Contributor Covenant, version 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).
